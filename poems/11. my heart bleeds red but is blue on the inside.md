

# my heart bleeds red but is blue on the inside
## self destructs every month


broke a toenail, bruised a knee

sick every monday

my body self destructs every month

but stray cat doesn’t let me leave

there’s fractals in cauliflowers

sounds only you can hear  

but they’re gone when you turn your head

did a tarot reading in the theatre

the fire comes back only at night

in the morning it is burns like a thousand suns

neurodivergence or insanity

it is a dormant monster of 5 years

and lives in the depression grey hoodie

trying to fit back in hurts more than growing pains

the vessel is dysfunctional

always catastrophizing

the afterwardsness of being the perfect victim

anxious to avoidant

I locked myself in

the worst of both worlds

my heart bleeds red but is blue on the inside
