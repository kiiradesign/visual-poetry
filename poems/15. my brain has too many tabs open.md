

# my brain has too many tabs open
## and uses four browsers

all the signs were already written  
and the lines were blurred  
agree or disagree  
doesn’t matter  
you gotta defuse the time bomb

grab me by the ankles  
but I’ll stand on the  
shoulders of giants   
it is my pedestal  
until I drop to the ground  
self-fulfilling prophecies  
can never be wrong

caught a cold in the summer  
you’re spinning around  
and heating up your head  
the insides are burning  
you can’t feel your teeth  
or taste the lipstick  
your thumb is bleeding  
and you’re scared of doorbells

my brain has too many tabs open  
and it uses four browsers  
on the verge of failure  
the time is always running out  
  
two days had more moments than two years  
you only lose what you don’t deserve  
the playlist doesn’t make me feel anything

start over in April  
and it will rain
