

# manic pixel nightmare
## if you're a woman, you're only half the man


if you’re a woman, you’re only half the man 

paying double the price

the air is toxic 

the water is contaminated 

there’s a drone over your head

the orchids are falling dead

the sky lost its colors

the music won’t play

sneeze blood

there will be signs

nothing is random 

let the wind move your hair 

and the cold bite your bones

it is not a poem, it is a cipher

we all have scars we want to hide,

on our bellies and hearts

it is all a sojourn

but don’t trust the timer

the cards never lie

don’t force it

write like a woman 

make the art

be the muse 

sever the art from the artist

tame the monster 

find your faith

respect your ancestors

CRASH OUT
