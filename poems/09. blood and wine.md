
# blood and wine
## pick your poison

everything was perfect

Lorde in the background 

wine in the bathwater

then everything broke 

blood in the shower

shedding it all 

Lilith would be so proud 

drink fire or burn your lungs

pick your poison 

just like the cards said

queen of denial

my hands were too heavy for you to hold 

and you scared me away

the night is a snake 

the cold wind will bite you, 

and then swallow you whole
