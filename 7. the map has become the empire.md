# the map has become the empire
## 15 minutes of fame



I never believed anything you ever said 

I don’t know why I stayed

I bought myself the flowers

and a vase to hold them


figure out all the tech stuff

hike in your work shoes

leave your work bag in the forest

become a naturalist

drive into the sunset forever


impulsively buy a saree 

and learn to wear it 

get yourself a lotus 

find a kitten in the dark

party with strangers

no milk no caffeine

nine inch nails 

twin with your bestie

get flowers from the girlies

move out again 

the most hated road in the city is empty

three paper cuts in a day

movie night with friends

diwali on a digicam 

pet the dragonfly eating an ant

the map has become the empire

do nothing for the algorithm 

do everything ironically

15 minutes of fame

there’s runway left for the runaway

the girl on the train reads a book now
