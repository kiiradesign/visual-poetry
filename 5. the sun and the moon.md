# the sun and the moon

## and the eclipse



the sun is my father  
the moon is my mother  
and the earth is my home

the sun was blindingly harsh  
and barely there on the cloudy days  
the moon was always in phases  
I never fully understood

and when they both clashed  
there were eclipses  
leaving me in the dark

I searched for a home  
in all the wrong places  
and in all the wrong people

I got lost in the woods  
pierced myself with metal  
I burned up my insides  
bled through my nose  
cried my heart out

until I found a rhythm  
in seasons  
in life and death  
and the rebirth

I tended to my garden  
and the jasmines bloom at night  
golden sunshine wakes me up  
and moonlight spills in through my window

the faeries came to me  
and took all my worries away
