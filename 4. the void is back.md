# the void is back 

## it is where all the forgotten things go

the void is back 

it is where all the forgotten things go

and the home of the voices in my head

the void is closer to the surface 

slithering under my skin 

to fish it out 

pierce me with metal 

see things with eyes closed

spill the wine 

it stains like blood

drink fire

smoke up the insides 

suck the energy

lick your lips

poison all the memories

save my soul
