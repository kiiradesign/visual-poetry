
# broken branch on the power lines
## unintentional ecobrutalism

watch the wind walk towards you
sunlight through a tree and a window, 
reflected on the floor, hitting the wall
ice cold water from the hot water tap
can't see tears when you cry in the rain



block all the ghosts from your past
rearrange furniture in the middle of the night
touch all bugs and fix them 
pet the moth 
attract the entangled butterflies



blanket of microplastics
constellation of pimples
matryoshka brain
suspended like a broken branch on the power lines
unintentional ecobrutalism
mid air cacao ceremony
decide when you can't decide
it's fair enough for the fairies
