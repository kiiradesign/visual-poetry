# I want to write happy poems one day
## but for now I only have escapism


delete the letter

and the film

erase the memories

don’t repeat the mistakes

can't waste your youth when you

arrest your development

press digital flowers

use chemical vanilla to mask it all

all play and no work

makes a jack of no trades

on some days i’m not alive

the legs are shaking

things are too heavy to hold

the brain doublethinking

with the writer’s fever

shadows crawling on the walls

force shut the eyes

the moment you become someone

is the point of no return

you can leave hell but it won't leave you

i want to write happy poems one day

but for now I only have escapism

the birds turn purple in spring

take me to the mountains

to prevent a structural collapse
