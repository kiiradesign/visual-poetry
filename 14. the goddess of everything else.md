## for when the times are dark

morpheus and calliope 
fight each other to death every night
a nightmare before you fall asleep
the recipe for this disaster is 
caffeine, milk and nicotine

you're in chaos but always chasing clarity
dressed in black but you only hear in colors 

you never realise what you are becoming 
making friends with the enemies
greatness can never be planned
but you can always set your heart on fire

don't work for the middleman
building a strong foundation of lies
written by a machine that never sleeps
fixing things no one asked for

resurrect after two months
in three months it will turn into poison

you feel like yourself on Tuesdays
for when the times are dark, 
and the sun in your eyes,
you must do everything else
